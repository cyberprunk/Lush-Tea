Your Daily Ritual

The Art of the Perfect Brew

Each Lush Tea blend is crafted with a specific brewing ritual in mind. Follow our guide for the ultimate experience.

01

Heat Fresh Water

Bring fresh filtered water to your tea's ideal temperature 85°C for green teas, 95°C for black and herbal blends.

02

Warm Your Cup

Pour a small amount of hot water into your cup, swirl, and discard. This ensures consistent temperature throughout the brew.

03

Steep to Perfection

Place your Lush Tea bag and pour over the hot water. Steep for 3–5 minutes. Never squeeze the bag.

04

Savour the Moment

Remove the tea bag gently, breathe in the aroma, and take your first sip. This moment is yours.

01

Double-Strength Brew

Brew your Lush Tea bag in half the usual water volume at the correct temperature to create a concentrated base.

02

Steep & Remove

Allow the bag to steep for 4–6 minutes for a bold flavour that won't dilute over ice. Remove without squeezing.

03

Pour Over Ice

Fill a tall glass with high-quality ice and pour your concentrate over it. Watch the colours bloom beautifully.

04

Garnish & Enjoy

Add a slice of lemon, fresh mint, or honey to complement your chosen blend.

01

Brew Extra Bold

Steep your Lush Tea bag in 100ml of just-boiled water for 5 minutes. The intensity is key when mixing with milk.

02

Choose Your Milk

Steam or warm 150ml of your favourite milk — oat, almond, or full-fat dairy all pair beautifully.

03

Froth & Combine

Froth your milk to a silky microfoam, then gently pour over your tea concentrate in a pre-warmed cup.

04

The Lush Finish

Dust with the blend's signature spice — cinnamon for Chai, matcha powder for Green — for a café-worthy finish.
